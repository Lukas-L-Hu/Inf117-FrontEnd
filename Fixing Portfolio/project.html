Creating a four-year college plan is crucial for tracking student progress and ensuring timely graduation. However, many UCI students lack such a plan or fail to adhere to it. Many UCI students find it challenging to select required major classes due to prerequisite constraints and class scheduling.

Survey Analysis

To delve into this matter, we designed a survey using Google Forms. The survey comprises 16 questions aimed at gaining insights from UCI students regarding their perspectives on 4-year planners.

Competitive Statement

To explore what options are available for UCI students to create a 4-year planner, we performed a competitive analysis to stay ahead of the competition and better meet the needs of our target audience.

Personas

To understand more about target audience, we created personas to learn more about their needs and desires.

Storyboard

We also created a storyboard to consier a specific use case and their thought process of using a 4-year planner.

Conclusion

To schedule and track progress, most students use the schedule of classes and major requirements as a main resource to plan their schedule for the next quarter. It seems that most people use this because it is updated quarterly with the new schedule, so students are able to check their major requirements to see if the courses are available during that quarter.
